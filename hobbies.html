My Hobbies

About Me

Since childhood, I've had the opportunity to discover what I love and what I want to do. Early on, I started sports, DIY projects, and video games.

Handball

I've practiced many sports in my life, but I found my place in handball. By chance, I joined my city's team (Villeparisis at the time) on a whim as a goalkeeper.

In 2020, my team and I were at our best, winning match after match in the departmental tournament. Unfortunately, everything was interrupted due to Covid. Since then, I stopped team sports to focus on my personal projects, but I keep excellent memories and would be ready to start again.

DIY & Electronics

When I was young, I saw my father doing renovation work to earn money, so I had a whole workshop at my disposal. I started tinkering at a very young age, trying to understand how things worked, discovering for myself how electricity works.

Today, I'm able to build electronic devices to make my life easier using recycled materials. For example, I recently completed an audio switch, allowing me to choose which audio input is active and which audio output is active, with a potentiometer to adjust the volume and a built-in Bluetooth input.

Video Games

Also from a very young age, I was put in front of a computer screen and learned software development on my own, but at the same time, I relax with various video games. My favorite video game is Brawlhalla, 5 years playing and still the same desire to play.
